About

TakeAChanceOnMe is a simple, elegant random number generator that works across all your Apple devices. Generate random numbers from 1 to 999,999,999 with a single tap.

Built with privacy in mind - zero data collection, completely local operation, and a clean interface that follows Apple's design guidelines.

Multi-Platform
