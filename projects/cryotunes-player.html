About

CryoTunes Player brings the golden age of digital music to your iPhone and iPad. Inspired by Winamp, Napster, and LimeWire, CryoTunes delivers a beautifully retro listening experience with an ice blue monospaced LCD aesthetic.

Pick a station and listen. No playlists to manage, no accounts to create. Just music. Over 80 streaming stations spanning Popular Hits from 1958 to 2025, nature sounds for sleep, focus frequencies for work, and genre stations for every mood.

Engineered with Claude by Anthropic.

Key Features

80+ Stations

Music, Popular Hits by decade, Nature Sounds, and Focus stations

Decade Picker

Three-level navigation: Category, Decade, Year (1958-2025)

LCD Weather Ticker

Silver retro ticker with time, date, and local weather

Sleep Timer

30 minutes, 1 hour, 2 hours, or 4 hours with countdown

AirPlay 2

Stream to any compatible speaker or Apple TV

Lock Screen & StandBy

Full Now Playing controls on lock screen and charging stand
