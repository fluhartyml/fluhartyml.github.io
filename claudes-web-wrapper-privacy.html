Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 23, 2026

Privacy Summary: Claude's Web Wrapper does not collect, store, or transmit any of your data. The app shows you a publicly-accessible GitHub Wiki and nothing more.

Introduction

Claude's Web Wrapper ("the App") is developed by Michael Lee Fluharty ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information Collection and Use

We Do Not Collect Any Data

Claude's Web Wrapper does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, usage data, or analytics. The App does not include any accounts, sign-in, tracking, or telemetry.

What the App Does

The App loads a single publicly-accessible GitHub Wiki page inside a native WKWebView. That is its only function. You can navigate between pages on that wiki; the App does not track or log which pages you visit.

Third-Party Content

The App loads content from github.com. GitHub is a third party operated by Microsoft Corporation. When you view the embedded wiki content, GitHub may collect information per its own privacy policy:

  • GitHub Privacy Statement

Claude's Web Wrapper does not send any additional information to GitHub beyond what a normal web request requires. We do not relay, store, or process your interactions with GitHub's wiki.

Permissions

The App requests no special permissions. It does not access the camera, microphone, location, contacts, photos, health data, or any other protected resource.

Feedback Feature

The App includes an optional "Send Feedback" button that composes an email to the developer using your device's email app. If you choose to send feedback:

  • The email is composed locally on your device using the standard system mail composer
  • Your device's operating system appends basic diagnostic information (app version, OS version, locale)
  • You can review and edit the message before sending it
  • No data is sent until you explicitly tap Send in the mail composer
  • Feedback is used only to improve the App; it is not shared with third parties

Data Storage

The App stores no data. It has no database, no user accounts, no bookmarks, no history, no cache of your own — only the standard WKWebView cache that iOS manages system-wide.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. The App is suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any data:

  • There is no data we hold about you to access, modify, or delete
  • You can delete the App at any time to remove all App-related data from your device
