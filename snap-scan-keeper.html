Snap & Scan Keeper

Document Scanner for iPhone & iPad

Scan documents with VisionKit precision. Share instantly, save as PDF, or both. Custom albums, no ads, no tracking. 100% free forever.

Four Flexible Save Modes

  • Share Only - no Photos clutter
  • PDF Only - single documents
  • Both - maximum flexibility
  • Pages Only - individual images

Smart Organization

  • Auto-created custom albums
  • Separate PDFs and images
  • Easy to find and manage
  • iCloud sync compatible

Powerful Features

  • VisionKit document camera
  • Auto edge detection
  • Perspective correction
  • Multi-page support

How It Works

  1. Tap "Scan Document" - Opens native iOS camera
  2. Capture your document - Auto-detects edges and corrects perspective
  3. Add more pages if needed - Build multi-page documents
  4. Choose how to save - Configure in Settings
  5. Done! - Share immediately or find in Photos albums

Privacy First Design

Your documents stay on your device. All scanning and processing happens locally using Apple's VisionKit framework. We never collect, store, or transmit your documents or personal information.
